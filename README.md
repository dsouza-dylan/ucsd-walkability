# 🔱 UCSD Has No Center: Mapping Campus Walkability

An interactive scrollytelling data story exploring **walking distances across UC San Diego**. The piece asks a simple question: is UCSD actually walkable? The answer depends entirely on where you live.

---

## ✨ Features

* A scroll-driven story that reveals **how your college determines your commute**
* Interactive **Leaflet map** with animated walk-time lines to each gravity center
* **Ranked college breakdowns** showing closest and farthest colleges for each destination
* **Heatmap** comparing all eight colleges across all four gravity centers
* Synthesis layers that **build the full picture** one destination at a time

---

## 📊 Data

* **Estimated walking times** (in minutes) from each of the 8 colleges to 4 campus gravity centers
* Four gravity centers measured: Geisel Library, RIMAC Arena, Price Center, Trolley Station
* Colleges: Revelle · Muir · Marshall · Sixth · ERC · Seventh · Warren · Eighth
